USART
HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer)

Detailed Description

Universal Synchronous/Asynchronous Receiver/Transmitter (USART)

  • Universal Synchronous and Asynchronous Receiver/Transmitter (USART) is synchronus or asynchronus communication protocol.
  • It is capable of three different serial communication modes below.
    1. UART
    2. USRT
    3. SPI
