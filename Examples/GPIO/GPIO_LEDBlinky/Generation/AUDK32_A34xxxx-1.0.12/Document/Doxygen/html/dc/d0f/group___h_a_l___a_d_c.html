ADC
HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer)

Detailed Description

N-bits Analog to Digital Converter.

  • Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC) converts an analog signal into a digital signal. It has N-bit resolution and multiple indedpendent channels on which the converting operates.
  • Moreover, it is triggered by the specific interrupt of Timer or MPWM sampling a voltage level on the channel.
