QEI
HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer)

Detailed Description

Quadrature Encoder Interface (QEI)

  • Quadrature Encoder Interface (QEI) generates a mechanical position signal.
  • It is capable of detecting position and speed of rotating motion systems such as Motor.
