VREFBUF
HAL (Hardware Abstraction Layer)

Detailed Description

Cyclic Redundancy Check.

  • Voltage Reference Buffer (VREFBUF) provides a stable reference voltage for ADC, DAC.
  • It improves measurement accuracy by reducing the influence of supply voltage and noise on analog conversions.
